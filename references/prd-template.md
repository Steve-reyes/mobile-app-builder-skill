**Priority definitions:**
- **P0:** Launch blocker — ship or die
- **P1:** High value — ship within first 2 months post-MVP
- **P2:** Nice to have — ship within 6 months
- **P3:** Future — backlog

## 6. Success Metrics & KPIs

### Activation
- % users who complete onboarding
- Time to first core action

### Engagement
- DAU / MAU
- Sessions per day per user
- Core action completion rate

### Retention
- D1 / D7 / D30 retention
- Weekly returning users

### Revenue
- Conversion rate (free → paid)
- ARPU / ARPPU
- LTV / CAC ratio

### Health
- Churn rate (monthly)
- Net Promoter Score (NPS)
- Crash-free rate
